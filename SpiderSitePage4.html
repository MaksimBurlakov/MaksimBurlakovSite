Velvet Worms

  • Cutest Hunter

    Despite their cute appearence Velvet worm is a very dangerous predetor that has a built in glue guns that helps them slow down and stop their prey from escaping.

  • Capable

    Biologists found out that Velvet worms have small but highly complex brains, and that they even might be capable of exhibiting social behavior.

  • Rare to find

    Velvet worms are pretty rare since they are also mostly nocturnal animals, so we know little about them but little by little we are starting to learn more about them, like that they prefer moist environment, that they like to eat termites,woodlice and small spiders,we also know that they actually have been around for 400 million years!
